# SEC COMMENT-LETTER — outline & working draft
### Track A, step 2 (per `COALITION_MAP.md`)

**Purpose.** A *rulemaking comment letter* (not a complaint about one issuer) arguing for disclosure
rules that make founder-controlled, absorb-and-list structures legible to the public — using the
Musk-entity record as the worked example. Viewpoint-neutral; pro-disclosure; no allegation of fraud.

> **Two different SEC channels — don't confuse them:**
> - **Rule comment** (this doc): public policy input on an open rulemaking → portal at
>   **sec.gov/rules** (or email **rule-comments@sec.gov** citing the File No.). Becomes public record.
> - **Tip/complaint about a specific issuer**: **tips.sec.gov** (TCR system). *Not* this letter.
>   Keep the policy argument and any issuer-specific concern in separate channels.

> **Before filing:** confirm there is an **open rulemaking / concept release** to attach to (e.g.,
> dual-class / controlled-company disclosure, IPO lock-up transparency, related-party disclosure, SPAC/
> de-SPAC analogs). If none is open, file to the **Investor Advocate** or as an unsolicited
> rulemaking petition (Rule 192 petition). **Verify the live File No. before sending.** `?`

## Letter structure (target ≤ 6 pages + dossier as exhibit)

**1. Header & identity.** Name, Watts Advisor affiliation + required IAR/firm disclosure line; "submitted
as public comment"; File No. [____]. *(WSP review required before submission.)*

**2. Summary of the ask (½ page).** "We support [rulemaking]. We urge five disclosure enhancements for
controlled-company IPOs and common-control combinations, illustrated by a fully-sourced record. We
allege no wrongdoing; we identify *legibility gaps* the current regime permits."

**3. The legibility gap, with a worked example (2 pages).** For each of the five findings, state the
**investor-protection gap** the rules currently allow — anchored to primary sources:
   - **Blended histories / no standalone financials** of absorbed units (SolarCity→Tesla `b`;
     xAI/X→SpaceX `b`). *Gap:* common-control recasting can erase the standalone track record a buyer
     is implicitly pricing.
   - **No line-item use of proceeds** when IPO cash repays inherited acquisition debt (Twitter→SpaceX
     ~$13B → $30.3B; IPO repays $18.9B + $1.16B penalty `b`/`c`). *Gap:* buyers can't see how much of
     "growth capital" is actually refinancing.
   - **Related-party pricing opacity** (Tesla 2025 proxy ~$235M/yr inter-entity flows incl. ~$191M
     xAI Megapacks; director-on-both-sides `b`). *Gap:* "no less favorable than third party" is
     asserted, not demonstrated.
   - **Lock-up/float-release legibility** (SPCX <5% float, staircase unlocks `b`). *Gap:* retail can't
     easily see the supply calendar that drives post-IPO price.
   - **Disclosure-channel control** (Tesla's 2013 designation of a personal account as the Reg FD
     channel; 2018 outcome, SEC PR 2018-226 `c`). *Gap:* Reg FD permits a personal, controlled channel
     to be the official wire.

**4. Why it matters now (½ page).** Record IPO; float <5%; ~36% economics vs ~82% vote; the same
structure recurs across vehicles a decade apart — i.e., this is a *pattern* the rules should address,
not a one-off.

**5. The five proposed enhancements (1 page).**
   1. Require **standalone audited financials** for any unit absorbed via common control within N years
      pre-listing.
   2. Require a **line-item use-of-proceeds** reconciliation when proceeds retire pre-existing /
      inherited debt.
   3. Require a **related-party registry** with the **pricing-basis methodology** (not just amounts).
   4. Require a **standardized lock-up/float-release calendar** in plain language in the prospectus.
   5. Require **sunset disclosure** (and consider listing-standard sunsets) for super-voting classes.

**6. Anticipated objections, answered (½ page).** "Already disclosed" → *disclosed ≠ legible; we ask
for structure, not more pages.* "Chills founder structures" → *neutral, applies to all controlled
issuers, asks only for clarity.* "You're talking your book" → *the asks help every long-term holder;
we take no position on the securities.*

**7. Close + exhibit.** Attach `STRUCTURE_BRIEF.md` and the dossier (figures linked to EDGAR). Offer to
brief staff.
